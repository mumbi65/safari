Malikale Kenya Presents:

4 DAYS LAKES NAKURU & NAIVASHA AREA ATTRACTIONS

Day 1: Nairobi – Lake Nakuru National Park: 160 km: Approx.3hrs

Meal plan: Breakfast,lunch,dinner
0700hrs:Pick up from Nairobi hotel / Nairobi Airport Depart for the lake Nakuru national park. An easy scenic sightseeing drive via the great rift valley escarpment with views of awesome scenery There shall be stops to marvel at the dramatic fall of the great rift valley escarpment. We shall stop at the Freshwater Lake Naivasha for an exciting boat ride to view hippos, exotic birds and small game. This shall be followed by guided walking safari at the Crescent Island Later proceed to the Lake Nakuru arriving during the late morning. The Lake Nakuru national park is home to black and white rhino, lions, warthogs, giraffes, zebra and other small game. It also hosts large number of bird species including Pelicans and sometimes a sizable Flamingo population. Afternoon shall be spent on bird and game watching drives around the Lake. Overnight at your lake Nakuru national park safari lodge/ tented camp

Day 2: Lake Nakuru – Masai Mara Game Reserve: 300 km: Approx. 5-6 hrs

Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
A Short game and bird watching drive at the lake Nakuru national park. Proceed to the Masai Mara game reserve. There shall be stops at scenic places along the Nakuru – Masai Mara route Afternoon game viewing drives at the Masai Mara.

Day 3: Full Day – Masai Mara

Meal plan: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.
Today shall be a game viewing extravaganza! Masai Mara Game reserve remains the most exciting wildlife eco-system in the Natural world. It is host to the highest and most varied concentration of wild animals than any other wildlife park in the region. The scenic appeal of the tree studded savannah plains, the moderate climate and diversity of wild game species makes it a one stop shop for game viewing activities. We shall traverse the Masai Mara reserve on a full day of unlimited game viewing encountering the big five and more of the lesser game. Picnic lunch shall be by the Mara river as we watch hippos & crocodiles or maybe the great migration (July – October) Overnight shall be at our Masai Mara safari lodge/ Tented camp

Day 4: Masai Mara Game Reserve- Nairobi – 265Km – Approx: 6 hrs

Meal plan: Breakfast, Lunch.
Early morning game viewing drive at the Masai Mara game reserve. Depart Masai Mara in the mid – morning and proceed to Nairobi. There shall be a lunch stop along the way. Arrive Nairobi in the late afternoon, approximately 4:30 pm. Drop off at your Nairobi area hotel/ Residence or Nairobi airport.
